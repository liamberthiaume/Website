Who We Are

We at Building Bricks we take pride in our history. Building Bricks is the first company to have designed the kind of toy where you can combine blocks in order to build things. You may be more familiar with Lego, but don't be mistaken. We came up with the idea first. Unfortunately, our wonderful toys didn't recieve the credit they deserve until much after Lego stole the limelight. Nonetheless, our first owner, Roger Berthiaume didn't do it for the fame and neither do we. We believe that children deserve to play with toys that stimulate their brains and encourage critical thinking. We wish to increase both fun and learning everywhere we can. We are a family-owned company that focuses on balancing fun with learning over profits.
