Store

Here at Building Bricks, there are many options to choose from when deciding to buy one of our products.

For those who want to custom-build something, or need a spare piece, we offer the ability to order a custom set of whatever pieces you need. Prices start at $0.15 per Brick.

For birthday gifts, christmas presents, personal use or any other reason you can think of, buying a set is always the best option. It comes with an instruction manual and every piece needed to build your desired set. Sets vary in price, but average out at roughly $150.

We're proud to introduce to you our newest feature. custom made Building Bricks characters! All you have to do is send us a picture of your face and choose the desired body parts. Within a week, you should recieve your custom $20 minifigure in the mail.
