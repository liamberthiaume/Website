Building Bricks

Building Bricks is a critically acclaimed company known for their innovative and one-of-a-kind toys. Located in aylmer right by the Ottawa River, half an hour away from Canada's capital, Ottawa, Building Bricks is a family-owned company that's been passed down for generations. Since 1899, Building bricks has focused on quality, affordability and customer satisfaction. The current owner, Liam Berthiaume, hopes to keep that legacy going for years to come. Our aim is to entertain children and push their creativity to the limit with our beloved toys.

The caring staff down at Building Bricks would love to help you with any problems you are having so long as you make it to our Store during any of our store hours. If you are unable to do so, please fill out a form located on the "contact us" page. If you wish to learn more about us or wish to hear testimonials from real customers, please head over to the corresponding webpages. If none of our products are what you are looking for, we strongly suggest Lego and Cobi as good substitutes for our products.
