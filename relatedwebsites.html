Cobi

Much like lego, Cobi came after us, but that doesn't mean that they aren't half as good. where Lego focuses more on imaginative sets, such as starships and things from movies like Marvel or Star Wars, Cobi focuses much more on historical models. a few of them include tanks, cars and planes. we feel like Cobi scratches an itch that Lego falls short on, which is why we have decided to include them here.
